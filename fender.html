Fender app

Brief

Put micro-animations in the Fender app. Then use tracking to put the screenrecording on footage of an iPhone.

Concept

I started by looking at how other apps use micro-animations to give delight. Almost all animations were done in Atomic, with more complex animation done in After Effects and exported as a gif.
